Technical Reference Guide

DECODING PIXEL PITCH & CABINETS

Selecting the correct specifications is crucial for maximizing visual clarity and ensuring industrial-grade weatherproofing for harsh retail and outdoor environments. Zorko manufactures every standard pixel pitch — P1.2, P1.5, P1.8, P2, P2.5, P3, P4, P5, P6, P8, and P10 — as fully assembled modules or complete LED display cabinets.

Pixel Pitch (P) Specifications

Pixel Pitch refers to the distance (in millimeters) between the center of two adjacent LED pixels. A smaller number means higher pixel density, sharper resolution, and a closer ideal viewing distance.

Pitch (mm) Application & Best Environment Ideal Viewing Distance
P0.5 – P1.8 Ultra-Fine Pitch / COB: TV Studios, Command & Control Rooms, High-end Corporate Boardrooms, LED video walls for control rooms. < 1 to 1.5 meters
P2, P2.5, P3 Standard Fine Pitch: Indoor Retail Fronts, Shopping Malls, Indoor Event Stages, Corporate Lobbies, LED video wall installations. 2.5 meters to 3 meters
P4, P5 Transition Pitch: Large Indoor Atriums, Premium Outdoor Retail Facades, Close-up Outdoor Screens. 4 meters to 5 meters
P6, P8 Standard Outdoor: Street-level Advertising, Outdoor Stage Rentals, Mobile LED Vans. 6 meters to 8 meters
P10 and above Heavy-Duty Outdoor: Massive Highway Billboards, Stadium LED Screens, High-rise Building Facades. 8+ meters

LED Cabinet Engineering

The cabinet forms the structural backbone of your LED display. Choosing the right material ensures longevity, precise alignment, and protection against extreme elements.

🏗️

Die-Cast Aluminum Cabinet

Ultra-lightweight, high-precision cabinets created using molds. They offer seamless splicing (zero visible gaps) and exceptional heat dissipation without needing cooling fans. Perfect for high-end fine-pitch indoor retail displays, premium outdoor screens, and rapid-assembly stage rental setups.

🛡️

Iron (Steel) Cabinet

Heavy-duty, robust structure designed specifically for permanent, fixed outdoor installations. When equipped with industrial-grade weatherproofing treatments (IP65+), Iron cabinets provide the ultimate cost-effective, durable solution for massive outdoor highway billboards fighting severe sun, rain, and wind.

Frequently Asked Questions

LED DISPLAY QUESTIONS — ANSWERED

What is the price of an LED display screen in India?

LED display prices in India range from ₹50,000 for small indoor poster-style panels to several crores for large outdoor stadium screens. The main cost factors are: pixel pitch (finer pitch = higher cost), cabinet type (die-cast vs iron), brightness requirement (indoor vs outdoor), and total screen area. Zorko eliminates the middleman markup.

How long does an LED display screen last?

A quality LED display is rated for 100,000 hours of use — equivalent to over 11 years of 24/7 continuous operation. In practice, most displays run 12–16 hours per day, making the usable life 15–20+ years. Zorko LED screens are rated for 100,000 hours.

What is the difference between indoor and outdoor LED displays?

Indoor LED displays use finer pixel pitch for close-up viewing and lower brightness (800–2000 nits). Outdoor LED screens require 4000–8000+ nits brightness to be visible in direct sunlight, plus weatherproofing (IP65+ rating) to withstand rain and dust.

What is pixel pitch in an LED display?

Pixel pitch is the distance in millimeters between the centers of two adjacent LED pixels. A lower number (like P1.5 or P2) packs pixels closer together for sharper close-range resolution, while a higher number (like P8 or P10) is built for viewing from a distance, such as stadium or highway screens.

What is "nit" brightness in an LED display?

A nit measures how bright a screen is per square meter. Indoor LED displays typically run 800–2000 nits. Outdoor LED screens need 4000 nits or higher to stay readable in direct sunlight — Zorko's outdoor series delivers 5500+ nits.

Is an LED display waterproof for outdoor use?

Outdoor-rated LED displays use an IP65 (or higher) rated cabinet, sealing the electronics against rain, dust, and humidity. Indoor panels are not weatherproof and should never be installed in exposed outdoor locations.

What is the refresh rate on an LED display, and why does it matter?

Refresh rate is how many times per second the display redraws its image. A low refresh rate causes visible flicker on camera. Zorko's MaxPanel series runs at 3840Hz, so the screen stays flicker-free in photos and live broadcast footage.

What warranty do LED display screens come with?

Warranty terms vary by manufacturer and typically range from 1–3 years covering the LED modules, power supplies, and control system. Ask any LED display manufacturer for their exact warranty coverage and AMC terms before you buy.

Should I buy or rent an LED display?

Buy if you need a permanent installation — a storefront, stadium, or corporate lobby — since the cost amortizes over 100,000 hours of use. Rent if you need a screen for a one-off wedding, concert, or exhibition, where a rental LED cabinet is cheaper and easier to move.
