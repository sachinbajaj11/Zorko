Technical Reference Guide

DECODING PIXEL PITCH & CABINETS

Selecting the correct specifications is crucial for maximizing visual clarity and ensuring industrial-grade weatherproofing for harsh retail and outdoor environments.

Pixel Pitch (P) Specifications

Pixel Pitch refers to the distance (in millimeters) between the center of two adjacent LED pixels. A smaller number means higher pixel density, sharper resolution, and a closer ideal viewing distance.

Pitch (mm) Application & Best Environment Ideal Viewing Distance
P0.5 – P1.5 Ultra-Fine Pitch / COB: TV Studios, Command & Control Rooms, High-end Corporate Boardrooms. < 1 to 1.5 meters
P2.5, P3 Standard Fine Pitch: Indoor Retail Fronts, Shopping Malls, Indoor Event Stages, Lobbies. 2.5 meters to 3 meters
P4, P5 Transition Pitch: Large Indoor Atriums, Premium Outdoor Retail Facades, Close-up Outdoor Screens. 4 meters to 5 meters
P6, P7 Standard Outdoor: Street-level Advertising, Outdoor Stage Rentals, Mobile LED Vans. 6 meters to 7 meters
P8, P9, P10 Heavy-Duty Outdoor: Massive Highway Billboards, Stadium Screens, High-rise Building Facades. 8+ meters

LED Cabinet Engineering

The cabinet forms the structural backbone of your LED display. Choosing the right material ensures longevity, precise alignment, and protection against extreme elements.

🏗️

Die-Cast Aluminum Cabinet

Ultra-lightweight, high-precision cabinets created using molds. They offer seamless splicing (zero visible gaps) and exceptional heat dissipation without needing cooling fans. Perfect for high-end fine-pitch indoor retail displays, premium outdoor screens, and rapid-assembly stage rental setups.

🛡️

Iron (Steel) Cabinet

Heavy-duty, robust structure designed specifically for permanent, fixed outdoor installations. When equipped with industrial-grade weatherproofing treatments (IP65+), Iron cabinets provide the ultimate cost-effective, durable solution for massive outdoor highway billboards fighting severe sun, rain, and wind.

Frequently Asked Questions

LED DISPLAY QUESTIONS — ANSWERED

What is the price of an LED display screen in India?

LED display prices in India range from ₹50,000 for small indoor poster-style panels to several crores for large outdoor stadium screens. The main cost factors are: pixel pitch (finer pitch = higher cost), cabinet type (die-cast vs iron), brightness requirement (indoor vs outdoor), and total screen area. Zorko eliminates the middleman markup.

How long does an LED display screen last?

A quality LED display is rated for 100,000 hours of use — equivalent to over 11 years of 24/7 continuous operation. In practice, most displays run 12–16 hours per day, making the usable life 15–20+ years. Zorko LED screens are rated for 100,000 hours.

What is the difference between indoor and outdoor LED displays?

Indoor LED displays use finer pixel pitch for close-up viewing and lower brightness (800–2000 nits). Outdoor LED screens require 4000–8000+ nits brightness to be visible in direct sunlight, plus weatherproofing (IP65+ rating) to withstand rain and dust.
